Smart Borrowing Tips

Choose Shorter Tenure A 10-year loan vs 20-year loan saves you massive interest — often 40–60% less. Stretch only if cash flow demands it.
Make Part-Prepayments Even Nu. 5,000 extra per year can cut years off your loan. Druk PNB allows prepayments with minimal charges on home loans.
Reducing vs Flat Rate A flat rate of 8% equals roughly 14–16% reducing rate. Always ask for reducing balance equivalent before comparing offers.
Maintain Good Credit A strong CIBIL score (750+) can get you 0.5–1% lower rate on home loans — saving thousands over the loan tenure.
